What we do?

Penguin Adventure specializes in organizing exciting and educational study tours to New Zealand for Malaysian students. Our goal is to blend adventure and learning, providing unforgettable experiences that foster personal growth and cultural understanding. Through carefully curated itineraries, we offer hands-on activities, interactions with local communities, and exploration of New Zealand's diverse landscapes, all aimed at inspiring curiosity and enriching academic pursuits.
